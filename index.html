Features

Everything you need to read more

From personal tracking to social clubs, Chaptered has all the tools to make reading a habit you love.

🔐

Authentication

JWT-based login & signup with secure password hashing and persistent sessions across devices.

📖

Reading Tracker

Add books manually or via search. Log reading sessions with pages and time. Build streaks.

📊

Dashboard

Active books overview, weekly reading insights, and quick session logging all in one view.

📚

In-Browser Reader

Upload a PDF and read it directly in Chaptered — no downloads, no external apps needed.

👥

Book Clubs

Create or join clubs with invite codes. Track member reading progress together.

💬

Real-Time Chat

Socket.io powered messaging with persistent history and timestamps inside every club.

🗳️

Book Voting

Suggest books, vote within clubs, and let the community auto-select the next read.

🤖

AI Reading Coach

Personalized recommendations, spoiler-free discussions, and an intelligent reading assistant.

📱

Mobile App

React Native mobile app, push notifications, video book club meetings, and reading challenges.

How it Works

Up and reading in minutes

Sign up, add your books, and start your reading journey with your community.

1

Create an Account

Sign up in seconds with a secure JWT-authenticated account that keeps your data private.

2

Add Your Books

Enter title, author, genre and pages. Optionally upload a PDF to read it right inside Chaptered.

3

Join a Club

Create or join a book club with an invite code. Discuss, vote on next reads, and track progress together.

4

Build Your Streak

Log daily sessions, watch your streaks grow, and see your weekly reading insights on the dashboard.

Why Reading Matters

The quiet superpower

Reading isn't just a hobby — it's one of the most powerful habits you can build.

🧠

Sharpens Your Mind

Regular reading keeps the brain active, improves memory retention, and slows cognitive decline over time.

💬

Builds Empathy

Fiction readers consistently score higher on empathy tests — stepping into characters' lives broadens your emotional range.

😌

Reduces Stress

Just six minutes of reading can reduce stress levels by up to 68% — more effective than music or a walk.

✍️

Improves Writing

Reading widely exposes you to different styles, vocabularies, and structures that naturally enrich your own writing.
